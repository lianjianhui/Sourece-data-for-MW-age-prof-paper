This repository includes the age and colour profiles of the Milky Way presented in our paper (Lian et al. accepted).

The raw and integrated profiles of the Milky Way from R=0 to 20 kpc are included in agev-int-260306.txt.  The predicted optical color profiles according to 
the integrated age profile is included in MW_colors_prof_260306.txt. Ages are in units of Gyr and colors are in units of AB mag.
