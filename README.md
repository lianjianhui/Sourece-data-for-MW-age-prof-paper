This repository includes the age and colour profiles of the Milky Way as well as colour profiles of nearby disk galaxies presented in our paper (Lian et al. accepted).

The raw and integrated profiles of the Milky Way from R=0 to 20 kpc are included in agev-int-260306.txt. The predicted optical colour profiles according to 
the integrated age profile is included in MW_colors_prof_260306.txt. Ages are in units of Gyr and colours are in units of AB mag. nearby-downbending-colour-profiles.zip includes the colour profiles for a sample of 71 nearby type-II disk galaxies. Each fits file includes the data for one galaxy.
